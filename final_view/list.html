Course description :
Learning - Online Content Management

Integrate web-based training (WBT) into the LMS
Add and edit content objects
Create online and blended items
Describe training standards, including SCORM and AICC

Course Goals
This course provides an overview of the concepts and terminology associated with online content management in the SuccessFactors Learning Management System (LMS). Administrators will gain basic skills in integrating online content and the LMS to add and modify content objects and blended items.

Prerequisites
Essential: HR860E – Learning Management System
Recommended: None
